Your Parking Recommendation (in seconds)

This page helps you decide before you arrive at DFW, so parking feels simple instead of stressful. Use the chatbot below to get a recommendation based on your trip length and what you care about most. Once you have your pick, the goal is simple: prebook your spot so your trip starts smoothly.

What “cheapest” usually means

If you’re traveling for several days, a value lot can save money while still being reliable.

What “closest” usually means

If time matters most, choose a near-terminal option so you’re walking less and moving faster.

What “covered” usually means

Covered parking is great for weather peace of mind—especially during storms or summer heat.

Pro tip

Prebooking is your “plan A.” It reduces last-minute stress and helps you start your trip on time.
